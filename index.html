Classic Krabby Pizza

  1. Preheat oven to 475°F (245°C).
  2. Roll out the pizza dough and brush lightly with olive oil and garlic.
  3. Sprinkle mozzarella cheese evenly over the dough.
  4. Distribute the crab meat over the cheese and add a sprinkle of red pepper flakes if desired.
  5. Bake for 10-12 minutes until the crust is golden and cheese is bubbly.
  6. Remove from the oven and garnish with fresh parsley and lemon zest before serving.

Krabby Alfredo Pizza

  1. Preheat oven to 475°F (245°C).
  2. Spread Alfredo sauce evenly over the rolled-out pizza dough.
  3. Sprinkle mozzarella cheese, followed by the crab meat and shrimp.
  4. Bake for 10-12 minutes, until cheese is melted and the crust is golden.
  5. Garnish with fresh basil leaves and cracked black pepper before serving.

Spicy Krabby Pizza

  1. Preheat oven to 475°F (245°C).
  2. Spread marinara sauce on the dough and layer with mozzarella cheese.
  3. Sprinkle crab meat evenly, then add jalapeño slices and red pepper flakes.
  4. Bake for 10-12 minutes or until the crust is crispy and cheese is melted.
  5. Garnish with chopped cilantro for a fresh, spicy kick.

Garlic Butter Krabby Pizza with Arugula

  1. Preheat oven to 475°F (245°C).
  2. Brush pizza dough with garlic butter and sprinkle mozzarella cheese on top.
  3. Add crab meat, then bake for 10-12 minutes or until cheese is golden and bubbly.
  4. Remove from the oven and add a handful of fresh arugula.
  5. Drizzle with lemon juice and sprinkle with Parmesan cheese before serving.
